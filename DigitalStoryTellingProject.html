My first semester experience as a CCIS student at Northeastern University.

Meaningful CS1200 Lessons

Time Management & Prioritization

I found the time management & prioritization lesson to be super meaningful in helping me find a balance between school, studying, extracurriculars, social life, and sleep. At first, planning an entire month to the hour seemed like an extremely daunting task, but it actually proved to be a great way to see how my entire month looked and I felt significantly less stressed. I will admit that I did not use this method for the rest of the months in the semester, but doing it once was still helpful nonetheless because I was familiar with how long certain assignments take me to complete and when I have ample time in my schedule to do them. I think that this lesson was given at a great time because it was right when the assignments started picking up in difficulty and assessments started becoming more regular.
